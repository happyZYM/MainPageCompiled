Hello, I’m Yumin Zhuang (also known as Astricaelus, a Latin translation of my Chinese name 庄裕旻). I am a third-year undergraduate from ACM Honors Class, Zhiyuan College, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majoring in Computer Science. Currently I’m working in the LUMIA Group under the supervision of Professor Zhouhan Lin.

With the aim of enhancing the intrinsic capabilities of AI systems, my major research interest is in the area of Large Language Models Algorithm. At present I am focusing on memory/RAG systems and exploring potential novel architectures.
